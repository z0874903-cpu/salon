The Atelier

A case built for unhurried attention

Every guest is seen by one artist, in one private suite, for the length of the appointment — no adjacent chairs, no shared mirrors. We keep the room quiet so the work can be exact.

Formulas are mixed to the guest, not the season's colour chart. Tools are sterilised between every single service. What we can't rush, we don't.

The Ritual

Every visit follows
the same four beats.

00 — Arrival

Consultation

Tea, a private consult on skin, hair history and the day's intention. No rush, no clipboard.

15 — Prepare

Preparation

Formulas are mixed to you in the suite while tools are laid and sterilised in view.

30 — Work

Treatment

The artist works without interruption — one guest, one chair, until the service is finished.

75 — Close

Reveal

A final mirror moment, aftercare notes, and the next appointment set before you leave.
